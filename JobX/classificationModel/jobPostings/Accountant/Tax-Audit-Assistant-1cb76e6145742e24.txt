Niraj Sanghavi & Associates, Chartered Accountants \- Mumbai, Maharashtra

|

**Job Summary**

 **JOB LOCATION - KANDIVALI**

Qualification Required: Graduates/MCom/Articles pursuing CA/ InterCA/ CA
dropped candidates

Whats in it for you?

We strongly believe in freedom of operation and that clearly reflects in our
approach for functioning.

 **Income-tax / Corporate Tax Tasks**

  * Computation of total income
  * Review of workings prepared by client
  * TDS Compliances
  * International taxation compliances

 **GST Compliance Tasks**

  * Computation and Making payment of taxes on timely basis
  * Review of workings prepared by client
  * Applying for registrations, amendments, etc
  * Filing returns
  * Applicable refunds
  * GST and erstwhile Indirect tax Audits
  * Handling Indirect tax Assessments

 **Audit Tasks**

  * Periodic audit including internal and statutory audits
  * Process audits
  * Due diligence activity
  * Comprehensive tax review.

 **Essential Skill sets required for the captioned post**

  * Communication skill and drafting skill in English is pre-requisite.
  * Advanced knowledge in MS Office and other soft skills
  * ERP Accounting knowledge is an added advantage as the post demands execution of activities for prominent large corporate.

Job Type: Full-time
