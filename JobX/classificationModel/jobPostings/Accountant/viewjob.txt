**Key Responsibilities & Accountabilities **

1) Preparation and finalization of financial statements as per IRDA
regulations.

2) Preparation for reconciliations of various Ledger with peripheral systems

3) Preparation of all regulatory reports relating to Finance functions.

4) Co-ordination of Statutory Audit / Internal Audit.

5) Preparation of monthly and quarterly management accounts, MIS and ad-hoc
reports as required.

6) Ensuring Balance Sheet Reconciliation for the Global books and local books
has been carried out  

  

## **Qualifications**

  

**Knowledge / Experience / Skills**

1) 1-2 years of experience in at handling MIS, and finalization of accounts of
the Company.

2) Candidate should be a Chartered Accountant.

3) Good understanding of Industry Practices in Insurance industry

4) Good working knowledge of ERP Accounting.

5) Good verbal and written presentation skills.

6) Knowledge of US GAAP will be an added advantage.
